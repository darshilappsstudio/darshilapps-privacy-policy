SECTION 2A

Storage Permission — Why We Need It

info
We explicitly declare that storage permission is NOT used to steal, transmit, or exploit your personal data. It is used solely to read and save WhatsApp status media on your device.

Because of how Android protects your files, our app requests different storage permissions based on your device's Android version:

phone_android Android 10 and older
We request standard READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions. This allows the app to find and read the temporary status files on your device and write/save them to your gallery.
phone_android Android 11 and newer
Android introduced stricter file protections. Therefore, we use the Storage Access Framework (SAF). We do not ask for broad storage access. Instead, we ask you to grant access only to the specific folder where statuses are saved.
flag
Why we need it: The sole purpose of our app is to act as a "Status Saver". To do this, the app must read the specific folders on your device where messaging apps temporarily cache status images and videos.
check_circle
Data Safety: We DO NOT upload these files to any server. We DO NOT access your general photos, personal documents, or files unrelated to statuses. All media processing happens entirely offline on your device, securely.

SECTION 11

How the Application Works

info
This section explains technically how the app functions, confirming it operates entirely on your device without any server communication or interception.

format_list_numbered Step-by-Step Technical Process

looks_one WhatsApp Saves Files on Your Device (Before Our App Opens)
When someone shares a status on WhatsApp, WhatsApp automatically downloads and saves those media files (images/videos) into a temporary local folder on your device's storage. Our app plays no role at this stage.
looks_two Our App Reads That Local Folder
Our app simply reads that same local folder where WhatsApp has already saved the files. We use Android's standard Storage Access Framework (SAF) or storage permissions to access only that specific folder — nothing else.
looks_3 App Displays the Media to You
The app displays those locally available files inside the app so you can preview them. No internet connection is used at this step.
looks_4 You Choose What to Save
If you tap "Save", the app copies that file from the temporary WhatsApp folder into your device's gallery/downloads folder. The file never leaves your device.

cancel What the App Does NOT Do

block
  • Does NOT intercept, read, or access WhatsApp messages or chats
  • Does NOT connect to WhatsApp's servers or use its API
  • Does NOT bypass WhatsApp's encryption or security
  • Does NOT upload any media or files to any external server
  • Does NOT access any files outside the WhatsApp status folder
  • Does NOT run in the background or monitor your activity
