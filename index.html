SECTION 2

Permissions Required and Why We Need Them

ℹ️
To function correctly, the Application requires the following permissions. We explicitly declare that none of these permissions are used to steal, transmit, or exploit your personal data.
💾 Storage Permission Varies by Android Version

Because of how Android protects your files, our app requests different storage permissions based on your device's Android version:

📱 Android 10 and older We request standard READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE permissions. This allows the app to find and read the temporary status files on your device and write/save them to your gallery.
📱 Android 11 and newer Android introduced stricter file protections. Therefore, we use the Storage Access Framework (SAF). We do not ask for broad storage access. Instead, we ask you to grant access only to the specific folder where statuses are saved.
🎯 Why we need it: The sole purpose of our app is to act as a "Status Saver". To do this, the app must read the specific folders on your device where messaging apps temporarily cache status images and videos.
✅
Data Safety: We DO NOT upload these files to any server. We DO NOT access your general photos, personal documents, or files unrelated to statuses. All media processing happens entirely offline on your device, securely.
🌐 Internet Access INTERNET

Why we need it:

  1. To load non-obtrusive advertisements (if applicable) which keep the app free.
  2. To send anonymous analytics and crash reports (e.g., via Firebase) so we can fix bugs and improve the app.
  3. To allow you to easily share links or media externally.
✅
Data Safety: This permission is NOT used to transmit your saved statuses or private files to our servers.
